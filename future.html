Future of Pakistan · پاکستان کا مستقبل
A hopeful, pragmatic vision focusing on education, renewable energy, technology, and youth leadership.
Vision

Inclusive growth & SDG alignment

Aligning development with Sustainable Development Goals: reduce poverty, expand education, improve health outcomes, and invest in sustainable infrastructure.

Energy

Renewable energy & resilience

Scaling solar, wind and hydro to reduce dependence on fossil fuels, stabilize power supply and enable green industry. Small distributed systems can help remote communities access electricity.

Tech

Tech, startups & skills

Invest in coding schools, affordable internet, and startup incubators. Support exportable services and R&D to keep youth in promising careers and attract international investment.

Youth

Youth empowerment & civic engagement

Programs that give young people leadership opportunities, vocational training, and community development roles will transform potential into progress.

Cities & rural balance

Sustainable urban planning + rural investment

Balanced policies that improve agriculture, irrigation and rural services while upgrading urban infrastructure will reduce inequality and improve livelihoods.

How you can contribute · آپ کا حصہ

  • Support local education & mentoring programs
  • Promote sustainable practices in your community
  • Volunteer for civic and health campaigns
  • Encourage entrepreneurship and learning
